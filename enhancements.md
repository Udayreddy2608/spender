# Mission-Based Budgeting App -- Advanced Enhancements

This document outlines high-impact enhancement features beyond V1. These
are structured by behavioral finance, automation, analytics, UX, and
productization layers.

# 1. Behavioral Finance Enhancements

## 1.1 Impulse Interruption Engine

When logging discretionary expenses: - 10-second pause countdown - Show
goal impact delta - Show remaining weekly budget visually shrinking -
Require confirmation

Track mood with each expense. Generate monthly: - Spending vs Mood
correlation - % of overspending linked to stress

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.3 Discipline Streak Tracker

Track: - Days under weekly limit - No-impulse days - Savings streak

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.4 Financial Identity Score

Score based on: - Budget adherence - Savings consistency - Impulse
overrides

Display 0--100 Discipline Score.
